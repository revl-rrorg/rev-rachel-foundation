The Arevakhach — Աrevakhach (Armenian Sun Cross)

An ancient 8-curved, spiraling emblem representing eternity, the sun, and the cycle of life. One of the oldest Armenian symbols, found throughout medieval monasteries and manuscripts. Its rotating arms evoke constant renewal — a perfect emblem for a foundation investing in Armenia's future.
